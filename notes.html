My Notes

It occurred to me recently that I should have a page on my site dedicated to my ideas, thoughts and opinions on Front End Development, as well as web development and the industry in general.

I love minimalism and always intended to keep this site concise, in both content and style. I've never wanted to bombard visitors with text, let alone with grandiose, sweeping statements about how "I loathe trends" and gimmicks in this industry.

Alas, here I am, writing things down that some people may find interesting (I hope), agreeable, and worth reading... while others may think and feel the complete opposite!

I would be remiss to not at least give people more of an insight and idea of who I am, how I feel and why I do what I do... so here goes!

Reflection on my Career - 01/08/2025

This may sound cheesy, but I really do love my job, and when I say job, I don't specifically mean my current role, which is great but what I really mean is front end development. 12 years in and I'm still not bored of it, I still get a kick out of figuring out solutions, learning new syntax and pushing something to production.
